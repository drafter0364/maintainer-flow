# Code of Conduct

Maintainer Flow aims to be a practical and respectful open-source project.

## Expected behavior

- Be clear, direct, and respectful.
- Assume maintainers and contributors have limited time.
- Keep discussions focused on evidence, tradeoffs, and project value.
- Welcome people who are learning while still maintaining a high technical bar.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.
- Publishing private information or security details without consent.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; restrict participation; or ban contributors when needed to protect the project.

If you need to report conduct concerns, contact the maintainers privately.
